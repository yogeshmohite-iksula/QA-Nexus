- (1) Session resume 11:30 IST — surfaced merge wave NOT done; Yogesh ping unblocks

**Cost-benefit:** 4 × 5 sec = 20 sec total pulse cost vs ~40 min Day-22 + ~3 min Day-23 branch-recovery cost. ROI scaling with each session.
