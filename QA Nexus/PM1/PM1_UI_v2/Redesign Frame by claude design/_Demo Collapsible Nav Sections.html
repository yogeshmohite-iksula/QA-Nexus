Collapsible Nav Sections — Demo

Click any section heading (PLAN / AUTHOR / RUN / ANALYSE / GOVERN) to collapse or expand its menu items. Default state: all sections expanded. State persists to localStorage.

How it works

Each section has a ▼ chevron icon on the right side of the section name.

Click section heading → items collapse, chevron rotates to ▶ (pointing right). Click again to expand.

Underline divider appears below the section label (the thin line stretching to the right of "PLAN", "AUTHOR", etc.).

Try it

Click each section in the rail (PLAN, AUTHOR, RUN, ANALYSE, GOVERN). Watch the smooth max-height + opacity transition. Refresh the page — your collapsed/expanded state persists per-section.
