Interaction rules

  • Drag past large top → rubber-band resistance (×0.4 movement)
  • Velocity dismiss: vy > 0.5px/ms downward → close regardless of position
  • Velocity snap: at release, current detent picked by position + velocity vector
  • Tap on backdrop → dismiss (scrim has pointer-events:auto when open)
  • Esc key → dismiss
  • Scroll inside sheet body locks drag handler unless grip is the touch source

Behaviour

  • ← → nudge by step (default 0.01)

  • Live re-filter: 100ms debounce on change event
  • Drag tracking: pointer events, no transitions during drag
